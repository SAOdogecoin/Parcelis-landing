How we calculate this.

Protection premium

We charge a dynamic premium based on order value. For a $85 AOV order, the average protection premium is $8.74. This increases linearly with order value.

Merchant margin

Growth plan merchants keep 73% of protection premium. That's $6.38 per order on average. Your actual margin depends on plan tier, order volume, and negotiated terms.

Opt-in rate

Most Parcelis merchants see 65–80% opt-in at checkout. We default to 73% based on aggregate performance, but your rate depends on offer clarity, messaging, and trust signals.

Plan recommendations

Starter (under 1k orders), Growth (1–25k orders), Scale (25k+). Margins improve with volume. High-volume merchants get better rates and a dedicated CSM.
