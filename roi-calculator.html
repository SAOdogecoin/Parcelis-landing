What this calculator counts.

Three categories of real cost that merchants absorb when they self-insure shipping losses.

Direct claim payouts

Refunds and replacements you issue for lost, damaged, or stolen packages. No carrier reimbursement arrives in time — you absorb the full amount.

Support overhead per claim

"Where is my order?" tickets cost 15–60 minutes each in CS time. Multiply that by your monthly loss volume and it compounds fast.

Carrier claim drag

Filing a carrier claim takes 30–60 days and rarely covers full value. You refund now, wait to be reimbursed — if you ever get paid at all.
