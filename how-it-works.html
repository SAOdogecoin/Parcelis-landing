Questions.

Do I need to do anything when a claim is filed?
No. Customers file directly through our portal. Parcelis handles resolution end-to-end — you get a notification when it's closed.
What carriers are covered?
All major carriers — USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL — plus regional and international carriers. Coverage is detected automatically at checkout based on the shipping method selected.
How does the customer file a claim?
Through a branded claims portal hosted by Parcelis. It takes under 2 minutes to submit. No merchant involvement required.
How long does resolution take?
5–7 business days. Professional adjusters handle the review. Carrier data is pulled automatically so your customer doesn't have to chase documentation.
What does it cost?
Free to install. No setup fee. Customers pay the premium at checkout — you keep a share on every protected order.
