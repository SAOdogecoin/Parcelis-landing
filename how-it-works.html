How Parcelis works.

From install to first payout — here's what shipping protection looks like inside your store.

Install on Shopify

Six steps. End to end.

01

Install in minutes.

One-click install on Shopify. Plugins for WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, and NEXT Commerce. Custom API for everything else.

02

Configure your settings.

Set your default protection settings and choose how the offer appears to customers at checkout.

03

Customers opt in at checkout.

A clean "Protected by Parcelis" line item. The premium is collected with the order and tracked separately on the receipt.

04

We monitor every shipment.

Real-time carrier tracking across all major and regional carriers. Automatic alerts when issues arise.

05

Customers file claims easily.

A branded claim portal hosted by Parcelis, matching your store's branding. Simple and quick to complete.

06

Resolved in 5–7 days.

Refunds or replacements handled by Parcelis. Merchant margin paid directly to your account.

This is what your customer sees.

One opt-in at checkout — your colors, your copy. Customers stay in your flow.
