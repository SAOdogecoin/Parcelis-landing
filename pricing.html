Pricing questions.

Who pays the premium?
Your customer. The premium is added to their order at checkout as an optional line item.
What does it cost me?
No setup fees. Customers pay the premium at checkout. You share in the revenue based on your plan.
How does pricing work?
Pricing is based on your store volume and needs. Contact us for a custom quote.
Can I disable it?
Yes, anytime. You can disable the checkout widget from your dashboard. Existing claims still resolve normally.
What about support?
We provide email support across all plans. Growth and Enterprise plans include priority support and dedicated resources.
