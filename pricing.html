What's covered.

Covered

  • Loss or theft in transit
  • Damage during shipping
  • Porch piracy and stolen packages
  • Weather-related damage
  • Carrier misdelivery
  • International shipping incidents

Standard exclusions

  • Perishables without cold chain documentation
  • Pre-existing damage at time of shipment
  • Fragile items not packed to carrier standards
  • Delays without documented damage or loss
  • Fraudulent claims

Pricing questions.

Who pays the premium?
Your customer. The premium — 2.5% of the package value — is added to their order at checkout as an optional line item. You pay nothing.
What does it cost me?
Nothing. No setup fees, no platform fees, no minimums. Customers pay the premium at checkout — Parcelis takes a flat cut and you keep the merchant margin.
How does my merchant margin work?
You earn a share of every premium collected on your store. The margin share depends on your plan and order volume. Contact us for specifics on your tier.
What's the maximum coverage per shipment?
$2,500 per box and $25,000 per shipment. Coverage limits are automatically applied at checkout based on declared package value.
Can I disable it?
Yes, anytime. You can disable the checkout widget from your dashboard. Any existing claims continue to resolve normally — your customers are not affected.
What carriers are covered?
All major carriers — USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL — plus regional and international carriers. Coverage is detected automatically at checkout based on the shipping method selected.
